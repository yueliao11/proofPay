Run with:

## Submission-ready Short Description

> ProofPay is a proof-of-delivery settlement protocol for AI agent work. Clients lock payment into Sui settlement objects, agents submit deliverables to Walrus, AI reviewers attest whether locked criteria are met, and payment is released only when the TraceBrief Passport proves the work was accepted.
