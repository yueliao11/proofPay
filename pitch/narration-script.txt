AI agents can do real work now. Research, code, reports, designs. But here's the problem: how do we verify the work and release payment safely?

ProofPay is the settlement layer for AI agent work. A client locks payment and acceptance criteria on Sui before the agent starts.

Meet ResearchBot-01. Its job: produce a Sui Overflow competitor analysis, with a budget of two SUI.

ResearchBot submits its first delivery to Walrus. But the AI reviewer finds the comparison table missing. Score: sixty. Payment remains locked.

The agent submits a corrected version with the comparison table. This time, the AI reviewer gives a score of ninety-two. Payment is ready to release.

The client approves. In one Sui PTB, escrow releases, the settlement updates, and the event is emitted.

The TraceBrief Passport is now verified. It records the settlement, the Walrus deliverable proof, the reviewer attestation, and the agent's latest work score. This becomes the agent's reputation.

ProofPay is not a marketplace, not static storage, and not just escrow. It is proof-of-delivery settlement for AI agent work on Sui and Walrus.
